About Lejweleputswa Chess

Our goal is to promote and develop chess as both a competitive sport and a tool for social development. We strive to create an environment where players of all ages—from Under-12 to Seniors—can test their skills, build lasting friendships, and reach their maximum potential

  • Regional Tournaments: We host regular events, including the Lejweleputswa Closed Championships, which serve as critical qualifiers for provincial and national stages.
  • Pathways to Excellence: We support our players in qualifying for prestigious events such as the South African Junior Chess Championships (SAJCC) and the Free State Top Schools competitions.
  • As a proud member of the Free State Chess federation, we adhere to the standards and regulations of Chess South Africa (CHESSA), ensuring our tournaments are professionally managed and our players are recognized nationally.
